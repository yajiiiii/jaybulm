About the universe

JAYBULM is an interactive artist album by Yaji.

This space turns album images into a floating 3D universe. Each cover becomes a visual particle you can rotate, zoom into, and explore like a living gallery.

This was just came out of a desire to make something fun and interactive with the artist album I liked. I hope you enjoy drifting through the JAYBULM universe as much as I enjoyed making it!
